## Background of the Charity

Wishaw YMCA is a community organisation that has recently pivoted to focus on digital inclusion and youth development through eSports. Last year, despite funding reductions, they secured £23k in donated equipment to upgrade PCs and now operate two eSports rooms and a podcast space.

They recently ran a successful pilot _“Mini League”_ involving 6 YMCAs (5 in Scotland, 1 in Ireland).

They have developed a unique badging system for their junior esports group to encourage positive online behaviour. Currently, this is managed on a WordPress site built by the Centre Manager herself, who has limited technical knowledge of the platform. They need professional help to stabilise and improve this system.

---

## Problem

The **“Pathways” programme** uses a gamified _“Football Card”_ system (5 main badges, sub-badges, XP) which is currently hosted on a self-built WordPress site.

Because the site was built by a non-technical staff member, it is difficult to update and maintain.

Currently, the Centre Manager must manually input data into spreadsheets and try to reflect this on the website. This is time-consuming and results in a user experience that is clunky and difficult for participants and parents to navigate. They need a solution that is easier to use and more accessible.

---

## Your Impact

The project targets local youth, guiding them from casual gaming to competitive eSports and digital careers. The pilot involved 6 YMCAs.

A better system will:

- Allow parents to engage more deeply with their child’s progress
- Enable the charity to scale the programme to more young people without administrative burnout

## Goal

### Desired Outcome

Development of a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** to replace the spreadsheet system.

The app should:

- Allow non-technical admins to easily log activities
- Automatically calculate progress and badges

The end state is to package this app as a **SaaS solution** that can be licensed to other YMCAs, creating a sustainable revenue stream.

### Measuring Success

1. Reduction in admin time for Emma
2. Positive feedback from young people and parents regarding ease of access

### Scope

**In Scope**

- UX/UI design for accessibility
- Admin dashboard
- Student/Parent portal
- Automation logic
- Database setup

**Out of Scope**

- Content creation for the badges (logic already exists)

### Why this issue?

The current manual process is the primary bottleneck preventing the programme’s growth.
